FACILITIES

Learn More

Library

A well equipped comprises of text books, law journals, news papers, periodicals & magazines with upto date reading facilities.

Moot Court

The college has its own well furnished Moot court for imparting practical legal training to the students guided by experienced practicing advocates having more than 25 years experiences of practice.

Computer Lab

The college has its own computer lab with internet facilities having wi-fi connections for the students

Auditorium

A spectacular auditorium is in the first floor of the college building inaugurated by the Hon'ble M.P. Mr.S.Sonowal is an another edition to its glory of the college.

Magazine

'Prastuti' - A college magazine is published annually

'Uttaran' - A wall magazine is also published at regular intervals

Legal Aid Cell

D.H.S.K law college has its legal cell with prominent legal practitioner as advisor of the cell. Moreover the college organised legal awareness camps to impart legal knowledge among the people. The District Legal Service Authority, Dibrugarh also organised several workshop on legal issues from time to time. The college also send students for the training of para-legal volunteers under the District Legal Service Authority Dibrugarh
